# Project 2 — Risk Register & Risk Assessment Programme

## Overview
Led end-to-end IT risk assessments across cloud environments (AWS, Azure, GCP) at PRMP Digital, populating and managing a formal risk register with owners, treatment plans, and governance reporting.

## Methodology

1. Scope Definition
2. Threat & Vulnerability Identification (Nessus, GuardDuty, workshops)
3. Likelihood & Impact Scoring (5x5 matrix)
4. Inherent Risk Rating (likelihood x impact)
5. Control Assessment and Residual Risk Calculation
6. Treatment Decision: Mitigate / Accept / Transfer / Avoid
7. Risk Register Population with owners and target dates
8. Governance Reporting in fortnightly reviews

## Risk Appetite
Risk appetite threshold: **12** — risks scoring above 12 require an active treatment plan.

## Risk Matrix

| | Impact 1 | Impact 2 | Impact 3 | Impact 4 | Impact 5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

| Likelihood 3 | 3 | 6 | 9 | 12 | 15 |
| Likelihood 2 | 2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 10 |
| Likelihood 1 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |

## Outcomes
- 40+ risks formally documented across cloud and on-premises environments
- 12 previously unrecorded third-party risks identified and treated
- Zero-trust deployment reduced unauthorised access risks by 35%
- Residual risk visibility provided to governance committee
