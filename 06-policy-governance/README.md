# Project 6 — Security Awareness & Policy Governance Programme

## Overview
Designed and delivered a security awareness programme and policy governance framework at New Horizon resulting in a 40% reduction in phishing incident rates.

## Security Awareness Programme

| Component | Frequency |
|---|---|
| Phishing Simulations | Monthly |
| Mandatory Security Awareness Training | Annual + on-boarding |
| Role-specific Briefings (IT, Finance, Executives) | Quarterly |
| Internal Security Bulletin | Monthly |
| Suspicious Activity Reporting Culture | Ongoing |

## Outcomes
- Phishing incident rates reduced by 40%
- Staff reporting of suspicious emails increased significantly
- Policy awareness scores improved in annual security culture assessment

## Policy Governance Framework

### Policy Development Process

1. Mandate Identification — regulatory obligation, risk decision, or business requirement
2. Stakeholder Consultation — IT, legal, HR, business operations
3. Drafting — aligned to ISO 27001, NIST, NDPA
4. Review Cycle — stakeholder comments, legal sign-off
5. Approval — governance committee sign-off
6. Publication & Training — policy published, acknowledgement recorded
7. Annual Review — mandatory every 12 months
